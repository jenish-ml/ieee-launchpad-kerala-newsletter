IEEE LaunchPad Kerala Newsletter

Introduction

IEEE LaunchPad Kerala is a premier job fair organized by IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn. It aims to connect talented engineering students and graduates with innovative companies in Kerala, fostering career opportunities and driving innovation in the region.

Event Roadmap

LaunchPad Kerala 2024 will feature a rigorous assessment process including technical challenges and online tests. Shortlisted candidates will have the opportunity to choose interview locations in Trivandrum, Kochi, or Kozhikode, enhancing accessibility for both candidates and companies.
